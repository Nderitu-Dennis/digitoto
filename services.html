Our Services

At DigiToto IT Solutions, we offer a range of services focused on empowering schools and communities by increasing access to technology. We believe that by providing essential IT services, we can help bridge the digital divide and create a more tech-savvy future for all.

Laptop Donation Program

We work with generous partners and volunteers to source and donate laptops to schools in underprivileged areas. Our goal is to ensure that students from all walks of life have access to modern tools that will help them learn and grow in a tech-driven world.

What We Do:

  1. Identify schools in need across Kenya, especially in underprivileged areas
  2. Partner with donors and organizations to acquire laptops.
  3. Distribute the laptops directly to schools to ensure they are used effectively.

IT Literacy Training

We believe that access to technology should be paired with the skills to use it. DigiToto offers IT literacy training programs to students and teachers alike, ensuring that the technology we provide is being used to its full potential.

Training includes:

  1. Basic computer skills for students (word processing, internet navigation, etc.).
  2. Specialized training for teachers on how to incorporate technology into the classroom

Technical Support and Maintenance

Once the laptops are distributed, our job does not stop. We offer continuous technical support to ensure the equipment remains functional and up-to-date. Our team is ready to assist schools with repairs, software updates, and general maintenance.

Our Services include:

  1. On-site and remote technical support
  2. Regular maintenance visits to ensure the longevity of the donated equipment
