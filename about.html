About Us

At DigiToto, we are a passionate community-based organization dedicated to bridging the digital divide in Kenya. Comprised primarily of volunteers who share a common goal, we have witnessed firsthand the staggering number of children who lack access to computers and the internet. In many areas, especially in the slums surrounding Nairobi, such as Ruai, Kibera, and Kayole—commonly referred to as Eastlando—families face the harsh reality of inadequate resources. Some of these children do not even have electricity in their homes, making it virtually impossible for them to engage with technology and develop essential digital skills.

We aim to empower these young minds by providing laptops to schools in need. We believe that access to technology is a fundamental right, not a privilege. By fostering IT literacy, we equip students with the tools they need to thrive in a rapidly advancing digital world. Each laptop we distribute represents hope and opportunity, enabling students to explore educational resources, connect with the global community, and ultimately become tech-savvy individuals prepared for the future. Together, we can transform lives and build a more equitable society where every child has the chance to succeed.
